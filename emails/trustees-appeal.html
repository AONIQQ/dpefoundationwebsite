A founding moment for the Foundation

Fellow Trustees,

For more than sixty years, the Delta Phi Epsilon Foundation for Foreign Service Education has advanced the study and practice of foreign service — quietly, and entirely through the work of volunteers. This spring we are launching the Foundation’s first organized fundraising effort, and it begins with the Board.

What we steward is rare: an endowment built from the Alpha Chapter House Plan, a growing slate of scholarships and awards for Georgetown students, and a lecture and programming series we are bringing back to life. Donor support lets us expand that work — and a strong showing from trustees signals to every brother that the people closest to this mission stand behind it with their own resources.

Your gift directly supports students and programs:

$250 Sends a Georgetown student to the Foreign Service Officer Test (Bleakley Scholarship).
 
$300 Recognizes service and innovation through a STIA Award, with Georgetown’s School of Foreign Service.
 
$500 Backs a future global leader (Stanley Weiss or Halleck A. Butts Scholarship).
 
$1,000 Helps revive the lecture series and expand student programming.
